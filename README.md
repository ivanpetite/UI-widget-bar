How to use?: 

Feature 1: Amovible Navbar.
Long press on the to left angle and drag the floating sidebar on the top, left, right, bottom side of the viewport, the navbar will automatically stick to the border.

Feature 2: Widget bar.
Long press on a widget located in the middle sidebar area will let you add, remove a function. Clicking outside the widget bar will confirm the modifications.
